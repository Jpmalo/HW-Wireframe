Bio

James Maloney


James Maloney is an avid developer and enthusiast of media in all its forms. Originally from Orange County, James is a Cinematic Arts and Technology graduate from California State University, Monterey Bay. He enjoys all components of the production process, but especially post production, namely video editing, motion graphics, and visual effects. Experienced across several platforms, James finds that many of the skills he has acquired translate to other media outlets, such as web design and development. James has both internet and general marketing experience when it comes to making a name for a brand, through presentation of various mediums in multimedia. In his spare time James enjoys composing music and acting in short films.
